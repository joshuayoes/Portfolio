About me

I am a recent graduate from Washington State University Vancouver with a bachelors of arts in Digital Technology and Culture.

From my program and various work opportunites, I have experience in web and mobile development, along with graphic design with a focus in web design and UI/UI design.

I've always loved technology. My parents always tell people about how I was fiddling with computers as early as 2 years old, sitting on my grandma's lap so that I could reach the keyboard. What I love about working in tech is the constant learning. I enjoy the challenge of continuously sharpening my skills and engaging in creative problem solving.

WSU Vancouver Events

This project is a redesign of the events timeline from the WSU Vancouver Engage RSS feed. I created this project as a way to highlight how many things are going on each week at WSU Vancouver and help increase the visibility of clubs and other events on campus.

Revolt Coffee

I created a custom website for a local coffee brand distributed by Corwin Beverage Company. I redesigned this site from a previous version, leveraging existing brand assets and limited code from the previous site. Introduced pagination, Mailchimp integration, and an AR experience.

Albi By Speak

I created a high-fidelity click-through prototype for Portland-based startup Albi By Speak. Albi is a smart AI assistant that transcribes meetings, listens and notes important parts of the discussion, and emails action items to your stakeholders. I participated in weekly design revisions with the CEO to ensure a polished product. I conducted user testing with target customers to maximize usability of the design. I ensured smooth prototype hand off to external firm for development.


DTC 101 Textbook

Web pages commissioned by the CMDC program as a part of their Open Education Resource project to create an open source textbook for their DTC 101 class. The idea behind these was to create interactive modules to help strengthen learning, taken advantage of the additional capabilities of webpages beyond ordinary textbooks.
